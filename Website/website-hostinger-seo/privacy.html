Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

This page explains what data QuickToolApp processes and how it is used. QuickToolApp is an informational utility site and does not require account registration to use core features.

Data Stored in Your Browser

QuickToolApp stores optional preferences in local browser storage, such as recent conversion history, favorites, saved workflow presets, palette settings, and loan calculator values. This data remains on your device and can be removed by clearing site storage in your browser.

Third-Party Services

Currency conversion requests may call the Frankfurter API to retrieve market exchange rates. Ad serving may use Google AdSense and related technologies. These providers may process request data and cookies according to their own privacy policies.

Personal Data

QuickToolApp does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data through calculator inputs. Do not submit confidential personal, financial, medical, or legal information through this website.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as features or service providers change. The date above indicates the most recent revision.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: support@quicktoolapp.com
